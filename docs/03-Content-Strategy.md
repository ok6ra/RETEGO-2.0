# 03 - Content Strategy

## Content principles
Every page must answer three questions:
1. What service does RETEGO provide?
2. What type of object or operation is it for?
3. What operational result does it create?

## Homepage content strategy
The homepage should explain the company as a systems integrator for:
- access control
- vehicle entry control
- barriers and gates
- turnstiles and intercoms
- CCTV and LPR
- industrial entrance automation

## Section content rules
- Use real business scenarios, not generic claims.
- Describe the problem first, then the RETEGO solution.
- Include practical examples such as logistics centres, production halls, administrative buildings and residential sites.

## Copy tone
- Professional and direct
- Czech-first terminology
- Specific and technical where needed
- Clear, not promotional

## CTA guidance
Calls to action should be specific and useful, for example:
- Nezávazná konzultace
- Získat návrh řešení
- Domluvit technický review

## Content governance
Maintain one shared vocabulary for:
- technologies
- services
- project types
- customer pain points
- CTA language
