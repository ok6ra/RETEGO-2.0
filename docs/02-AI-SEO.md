# 02 - AI SEO

## Search and AI visibility goals
The website must be easy to understand for both search engines and AI systems. The content should be clear, structured and specific.

## Required content practices
- Use one primary topic per section.
- Write concise paragraphs that explain the service, the use case and the outcome.
- Include concrete product and technology names such as NICE, Hikvision, 2N and dormakaba.
- Use clear headings with descriptive keywords.

## Structured data
Use schema where appropriate for:
- Organization
- LocalBusiness or Service
- FAQPage
- WebPage

## Semantic rules
- Use semantic HTML5 sections and headings.
- Prefer descriptive headings over slogan-only headings.
- Keep important service terms near the top of the page.
- Use consistent vocabulary across homepage, service pages and FAQ.

## AI-readability rules
- Avoid vague marketing language.
- Prefer direct statements about what RETEGO installs, integrates and supports.
- Make answers explicit: what problem is solved, how it works and who benefits.

## SEO priorities
- Target Czech service keywords such as přístupové systémy, automatizace vjezdů, turnikety, CCTV, LPR, interkomy and brány.
- Make service pages easy to crawl and easy to summarize.
- Ensure every important section has a unique heading and clear supporting copy.
